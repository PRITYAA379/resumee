About Me

I started my work journey during the lockdown, a time when my family was going through financial difficulties. To support them, I took up a job as a mask packer during the COVID-19 pandemic. It was a survival move, not a choice. After working for nearly a year, I began to realize I wasn’t building any skills in that role. I knew I had the potential to grow, but I needed a new path. That’s when a close friend advised me to develop my communication skills and suggested I join a call center.

For the next three years, I stayed in the call center industry, exploring different roles and improving how I interacted with people. But over time, I noticed something was missing—there was no real growth. The repetition, the fixed routine, and the lack of creativity started bothering me. I wasn’t able to express myself or create anything valuable. That same friend then introduced me to the world of digital marketing. And the moment I entered, something clicked. I finally found a space where I could merge creativity, communication, and tech.

Today, I feel completely aligned with what I do. From growing Instagram to 50k followers, to monetizing YouTube, and now building futuristic 3D resumes like this one, I’ve carved a journey that’s unique. If you're hiring me, you're not just hiring a person who knows digital tools — you’re bringing in someone who turns limitations into innovations. My resume isn't just a theme; it's a preview of the kind of creativity I bring to every project.
